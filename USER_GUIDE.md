# 📘 GrantMatch AI User Guide

## 👋 Welcome

Welcome to **GrantMatch AI**.

GrantMatch AI helps nonprofit organizations discover funding opportunities that align with their mission. Instead of manually searching through hundreds of grants, the application uses Artificial Intelligence to recommend the most relevant opportunities based on your organization's goals.

No technical or AI experience is required to use this application.

---

# 🎯 Who Is This For?

GrantMatch AI is designed for:

* Nonprofit organizations
* Community organizations
* Educational institutions
* Charitable foundations
* Grant writers
* Program managers

---

# What You Need Before You Start

You only need one of the following:

✅ A PDF containing your organization's mission statement

OR

✅ A written mission statement that you can paste into the application

Example:

> We provide STEM education and career development opportunities for underserved high school students through mentoring, workshops, and community partnerships.

---

# Opening the Application

Open the GrantMatch AI web application in your browser.

The home page contains:

* Mission Statement input
* PDF Upload option
* Grant Filters
* Find Grants button

---

# 📄 Option 1 — Upload a Mission Statement PDF

If your organization already has a mission statement saved as a PDF:

1. Click **Browse Files**
2. Select your PDF
3. Wait for the application to extract the text automatically

Once extraction is complete, the mission statement will appear in the text area.

No copying or pasting is required.

---

# ✍️ Option 2 — Enter a Mission Statement Manually

If you do not have a PDF, type or paste your mission directly into the text box.

A good mission statement should:

* Clearly describe your organization's purpose
* Explain who you serve
* Describe the impact you create

Example:

> Our nonprofit provides career readiness and STEM education programs for middle and high school students through mentoring, internships, and technology workshops.

---

# 📝 Mission Statement Guidelines

For the best recommendations:

✔ Use complete sentences.

✔ Include your organization's goals.

✔ Mention the people or communities you serve.

✔ Avoid abbreviations whenever possible.

The application requires a meaningful mission statement containing at least six meaningful words.

---

# 🎛️ Applying Filters

You can narrow your search using optional filters.

### Minimum Funding

Use this filter if you only want grants above a certain funding amount.

---

### Category

Examples include:

* Education
* Health
* Community Development
* Environment

---

### Funding Organization

Limit results to grants from a specific funding organization.

---

### Eligibility

Select eligibility requirements if applicable.

If you are unsure, leave these filters unchanged.

---

# 🔍 Finding Grants

After entering your mission:

Click **Find Grants**.

The application will:

1. Understand your mission.
2. Compare it with available grant opportunities.
3. Rank the most relevant grants.
4. Generate an AI explanation for each recommendation.
5. Display the results.

This process usually takes only a few seconds.

---

# 📊 Understanding Your Results

Each recommendation includes:

### Grant Name

The funding opportunity.

---

### Funding Amount

The amount available.

---

### Similarity Score

Indicates how closely the grant aligns with your mission.

Higher scores generally indicate stronger matches.

---

### AI Explanation

An easy-to-read explanation describing why the grant is a good fit.

---

### Grant Description

A summary of the funding opportunity.

---

# 📥 Downloading Your Report

Click **Download PDF Report**.

The report includes:

* Mission statement
* Selected filters
* Recommended grants
* AI-generated explanations
* Similarity scores

The PDF can be shared with colleagues or used during grant planning meetings.

---

# ⚠️ Common Issues

## My PDF uploads but nothing happens.

Check that:

* The file is a PDF.
* The PDF contains selectable text.
* The document is not password protected.

---

## I receive:

**"Please enter a meaningful nonprofit mission statement."**

This message appears when:

* The mission is too short.
* Random characters were entered.
* The content does not describe an organization's mission.

Provide a complete mission statement with meaningful information.

---

## No grants were found.

Try:

* Removing one or more filters.
* Lowering the minimum funding requirement.
* Using a broader mission description.

---

# 💡Best Practices

For the highest-quality recommendations:

* Use your official mission statement.
* Keep the mission up to date.
* Review AI explanations before making funding decisions.
* Consider multiple recommended grants rather than only the highest-ranked result.

---

# ❓Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the application automatically apply for grants?

No.

GrantMatch AI recommends relevant funding opportunities but does not submit grant applications.

---

### Can I upload multiple PDFs?

Currently, the application supports one mission statement at a time.

---

### Is my uploaded document stored?

No.

Uploaded documents are processed only to generate recommendations and are not permanently stored.

---

### Why do some grants receive higher scores?

Recommendations are based on how closely the meaning of your mission aligns with the grant description, not simply whether they contain the same keywords.

---

# 🛠️ Need Assistance?

If you encounter unexpected behavior:

1. Refresh the application.
2. Try another PDF.
3. Verify that your mission statement is complete.
4. Review your selected filters.

If the issue persists, contact the project maintainer through the GitHub repository.

# 🤖 About GrantMatch AI

GrantMatch AI combines modern Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques with Large Language Models to help nonprofit organizations discover relevant funding opportunities more efficiently.

The goal is to reduce manual effort, improve recommendation quality, and make grant discovery more accessible for organizations of all sizes.
